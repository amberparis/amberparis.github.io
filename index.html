Amber Paris Turner

Textured printed-surface designer

Currently completing a BA(Hons) Textile Design at Bath Spa School of Art

Amber Paris is a textured printed-surface designer. She takes inspiration from a variety of naturalistic and industrial themes to create exciting juxtapositions in print designs. Developing her print designs through Photoshop and Illustrator to produce compelling digital textile designs and screen prints.

This site will act as a digital showcase of Amber Paris Textile Designs. Starting with the Bath Spa School of Art Degree Show 2016.
